//Get me IP address of particular domain (guvi.in) 
//How do I find my CPU/memory usage of my server? 
//Test the connectivity between 2 nodes? 
//I have deployed an application in guvi.com:9000, and logs shows my app is running, but I’m unable to view the page. Check whether my port is open or not ?

1.Get me IP address of particular domain (guvi.in)
answer: nslookup guvi.in

2.How do I find my CPU/memory usage of my server? 
answer: ps -aux

3.Test the connectivity between 2 nodes? 
answer: ping guvi.in

4.I have deployed an application in guvi.com:9000, and logs shows my app is running, but I’m unable to view the page. Check whether my port is open or not ?
answer: telnet guvi.com 9000
